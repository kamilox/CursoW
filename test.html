What is an SEO Marketing Agency?

To us at P5 Marketing, SEO Marketing is a philosophical approach to marketing that focuses on attracting prospects to your website. This is opposed to the classic outbound strategies that are seen as an interruption by your potential prospects. SPAM email, random phone calls, and junk mail are typical examples of outbound marketing.

The term inbound marketing was coined by HubSpot - and SEO is the core of Inbound. We are active Gold Partners with HubSpot and employ their CRM and tools whenever we can. Many of these tools are free and save time and money.

As an SEO Marketing Agency, we attract prospects who are actively researching what you have to offer. We call this a considered purchase. Inbound works best when the purchase price, risk, or importance justifies the purchase research required to make an informed decision.

Search engine optimization (SEO) skills are a key component in Inbound marketing. We have been using SEO as part of our corer offering since the inception of the concept. It drives what content we develop. how we build your website, what blogs we write and so much more. The agency part of what we do is to offer all the services you need under one roof to succeed. A single point of responsibility for your online success.
